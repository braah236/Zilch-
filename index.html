About Us

Zilch is a family-run business based in the UK, passionate about creating delicious ice cream that is both low in fat and sugar-free. Our journey began with a simple idea: to craft a treat that everyone can enjoy without compromising on taste or health. At Zilch, we believe that indulgence should not come at the cost of well-being. Our ice creams are made using high-quality ingredients, ensuring that each scoop is rich, creamy, and bursting with flavor. We take pride in our innovative recipes that cater to those seeking healthier alternatives without sacrificing the joy of eating ice cream. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction drives us to continuously improve and expand our range of flavors. Whether you're looking for a guilt-free dessert or a refreshing snack, Zilch has something for everyone. Join us on our mission to redefine indulgence with ice cream that is as good for your taste buds as it is for your body. Experience the joy of Zilch – where every bite is a celebration of flavor and health.
